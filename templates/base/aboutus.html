About us

Knowledge Network Nepal (KNN) is established with one simple idea of exchanging books, with the intent of saving money but growing our knowledge. We all have experienced that the feeling of reading a physival book cannot be replaced by its digital form, but it has a negative effect on the environment and of course our pockets. KNN is here to minimize those consequences but most importantly to contribute towards the culture of sharing knowledge with the message “Gayan Badhe Badhacha”.

We are registered as a private company with a goal to improve the quality of education and human resources in Nepal. Our earnings will only cover our operating cost and the excess funds will be invest in our community. Our mission is to make our youth rich with knowledge and our nation rich of productive human resources.
